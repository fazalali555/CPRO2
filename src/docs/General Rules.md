
# General Rules & Calculation Standards
**KPK Education Department - Retirement & Pension Management System (RPMS)**

**Authority:** [EXTRAORDINARY] GOVERNMENT GAZETTE KHYBER PAKHTUNKHWA (06th September, 2021)
**Notification:** No. SOSR-II/FD/2-4/2021
**Guidelines:** Accountant General Khyber Pakhtunkhwa

## 1. Pension Types

### A. Superannuation Pension (Rule 4)
Granted to a civil servant who retires on completion of **sixty (60) years** of age.

### B. Retiring Pension (Rule 5)
Granted to a civil servant who:
*   Opts to retire after **25 years** qualifying service; OR
*   Attains **55 years** of age (whichever is later).
*   Is compulsorily retired by competent authority.

### C. Invalid Pension (Rule 6)
Awarded to a servant permanently incapacitated (bodily or mental infirmity) for further service.
*   **Requirement:** Invalidating certificate from Standing Medical Board.

### D. Family Pension (Rule 15)
Granted to the family of a Government servant who dies.

**Scenario 1: Death Before Retirement (In-Service)**
*   **Gratuity:** 25% of Gross Pension is paid as a Lump Sum (Commuted Value).
    *   *Formula:* `(Gross × 0.25) × 12 × Age Factor`
*   **Monthly Pension:** 75% of Gross Pension is paid to the family.

**Scenario 2: Death After Retirement**
*   **Family Pension:** 100% of the **Net Pension** (drawn by deceased) is paid to the spouse/successor.
*   **LTA:** Life Time Arrears (un-drawn pension) paid to family.

## 2. Calculation Rules (Chapter IV)

### A. Core Components
1.  **Qualifying Service (QS):**
    *   Minimum: **10 Years**.
    *   Maximum Cap: **30 Years** (for calculation).
    *   Service > 6 months counts as 1 full year.
2.  **Emoluments:** Last Basic Pay + Personal Pay + Senior Post Allowance.

### B. Formulas (Living Retirement)
1.  **Gross Pension:** `(Emoluments × QS × 7) / 300`
2.  **Commutation (35%):**
    *   Pensioner surrenders 35% of Gross.
    *   **Lump Sum:** `(Gross × 0.35) × 12 × Age Factor`
3.  **Net Pension (65%):**
    *   **Base:** `Gross Pension × 0.65`
    *   **Payable:** Base + Medical Allowance + Adhoc Reliefs.

---

## 3. Age Determination for Commutation
**Source:** Rule 13 & 15

1.  **General Rule:** Commutation is calculated based on **Age Next Birthday**.
2.  **Superannuation Exception (Rule 13-4):**
    *   For pensioners retiring on superannuation (exactly 60 years), the value is calculated on the basis of **60 years**, instead of 61 (next birthday).
    *   *Factor for 60:* **12.3719**
