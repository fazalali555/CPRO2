
# KPK Civil Servants Pension Rules 2021 & AG Guidelines
**Reference Documentation for Development**

## 1. Pension Formulas

### A. Superannuation / Retiring / Invalid Pension
Applicable when a civil servant retires alive (Age 60 or 25 years service).

*   **Qualifying Service (QS):** 
    *   Length of service in years.
    *   **Max Capped at 30 Years** for calculation.
    *   Service > 6 months is counted as 1 year.
*   **Emoluments:** Last Basic Pay + Personal Pay + Senior Post Allowance.
*   **Gross Pension:** `(Emoluments × QS × 7) / 300`
*   **Commutation (35%):** `Gross Pension × 0.35`
    *   **Lump Sum Payment:** `(Commutation Amount) × 12 × Age Factor`
*   **Net Pension (65%):** `Gross Pension × 0.65`
    *   **Monthly Payable:** Net Pension + Annual Increases + Medical Allowance.

### B. Family Pension (Death In-Service)
Applicable when a civil servant dies while still employed.

*   **Gross Pension:** Calculated as above (Max 30 years service).
*   **Gratuity (25%):** `Gross Pension × 0.25` (Paid as Lump Sum).
*   **Family Pension (75%):** `Gross Pension × 0.75` (Paid Monthly).
*   *Note:* No 35% commutation is calculated here; the split is strictly 25% Gratuity / 75% Pension.

### C. Family Pension (Death After Retirement)
Applicable when a pensioner dies.

*   **Family Pension:** 100% of the **Net Pension** being drawn by the deceased pensioner.
*   **Increases:** Admissible as drawn by the deceased.
*   **Medical Allowance:** Admissible to widow/widower (if not drawing their own).

## 3. Required Document Checklists

### List A: Superannuation / Retiring / Invalid
1. Physical verification of pensioner & Focal Person.
2. Forwarding Memo.
3. Pension Application (Form 6).
4. Pension Papers (signed).
5. Notification/Order of Retirement.
6. Last Pay Certificate (LPC).
7. Pension Contribution Certificate (Foreign Service).
8. Original Service Book (Page-3 re-attested).
9. NOC from Estate Office / Non-Govt Accommodation Cert.
10. Three attested photos.
11. Three attested CNIC copies (Self + Spouse).
12. List of Family Members (FRC).
13. Specimen Signature/Thumb Impressions.
14. Option for Commutation.
15. Bank Account Details (DCS Form).
16. *If Medical:* Invalidation Certificate from Standing Medical Board.

### List B: Family Pension (In-Service Death)
1. Department Cover Letter.
2. Physical verification of widow/heir & Focal Person.
3. Pension Application (Form 3) + 3 Photos.
4. Pension Papers (signed).
5. Death Certificate of Employee.
6. Last Pay Certificate (LPC).
7. Original Service Book (Page-3 re-attested).
8. NOC from Estate Office / Accommodation Cert.
9. Non-Marriage / Non-Remarriage Certificate.
10. Affidavit on Pension Form-V.
11. Single Widow Certificate (if applicable).
12. 3 Specimen Signatures.
13. 3 Sets Thumb/Finger Impressions.
14. 3 Lists of Family Members (Attested).
15. Non-Separation Certificate.
16. Guardianship Certificate (if minors involved).
17. 3 Descriptive Rolls.
18. 3 CNIC Copies (Beneficiary).
19. DCS Option Form (Bank).
20. CNIC Cancellation Certificate (NADRA) of deceased.
21. *If Daughter:* Unmarried/Widow/Divorce Certificate.

### List C: Family Pension (Death After Retirement)
*Same as List B, but:*
*   Requires **PPO (Pension Payment Order)** of the deceased.
*   Requires **Life Certificate** (that they were alive until date of death).
*   Does not require Service Book (usually already with Audit, though copy helps).

## 4. Adhoc Relief History (KPK)
*Used for calculating Net Monthly Pension*

1.  **Medical Allowance 2010:** 25% of Net Pension (Frozen at 2010 level for older, calculated on current for fresh).
2.  **Medical Allowance Increase 2015:** 25% of Medical Allowance 2010.
3.  **Adhoc Relief 2011-2021:** (Various merged/frozen rules).
4.  **Adhoc Relief 2022:** 15% on Net Pension.
5.  **Adhoc Relief 2023:** 17.5% on Net Pension.
6.  **Adhoc Relief 2024:** 10% on Net Pension.
*(Note: 2025/2026 rates subject to future budget notifications)*
